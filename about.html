When were we established?


  • Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves and can provide more marketable skills.

    • Our Goal


  • Our goal is to provide proper education to underdeveloped areas in our nation, encouraging unity and polishing hidden gems. We aim to create a stronger and more successful nation by nurturing our own people, leading not only to a better today but a better tomorrow as well.
